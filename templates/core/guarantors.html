About Guarantors

Guarantors are members who vouch for a loan applicant. They provide additional security for the loan and can be held liable if the borrower defaults.

Guarantor Requirements:
  • Must be an active member of the group
  • Must have good credit standing (no defaulted loans)
  • Cannot guarantee more than 2 loans simultaneously
  • Must have sufficient savings to cover potential liability
  • Subject to admin approval
